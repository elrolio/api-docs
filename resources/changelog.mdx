## Versioning policy

The API is currently at **v1** (`https://descriptapi.com/v1/...`).

During the open beta:
- **Breaking changes** receive at least 30 days advance notice
- **New features** are announced here and in the [Discord community](https://discord.gg/descript)
- **Bug fixes** are deployed continuously without version bumps

## Stay updated

- Join the [Descript API Discord](https://discord.gg/descript) for real-time updates
